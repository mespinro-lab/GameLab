### DYN-02 Â· familyReputation snapshot at birth, not at death
- **Reported by**: playtester-dynasty-builder
- **Source**: `src/life-tycoon/game.js:658` (generateChild), `game.js:961` (doSuccession)
- **Description**: `generateChild` snapshots `familyReputation` at the moment the child is born (`familyReputation: S.resources.familyReputation.value`). `doSuccession` restores from that snapshot (`S.resources.familyReputation.value = child.familyReputation`). Reputation accumulated between a child's birth and the parent's death is silently discarded.
- **Reproduction**: Gen 1 â€” have children at cycle 5 (rep=20). Spend rest of life farming reputation to 65. Succeed to gen 2 â€” reputation resets to 20 despite the 45-point gain.
- **Impact**: Invisible progress loss for dynasty-focused players; undermines the late-game reputation loop. Particularly punishing in Edat Antiga where high-reputation actions are gated.
- **Route to**: `gameplay-programmer`

### DYN-07 Â· Educar Fills teach cap hard-coded at 2, ignores maxLearnedSkills
- **Reported by**: playtester-dynasty-builder
- **Source**: `src/life-tycoon/game.js:347`
- **Description**: The child-teach eligibility check uses a hard-coded `< 2` limit: `(c.learnedSkillIds || []).length < 2`. Edat Antiga sets `maxLearnedSkills: 3`, so a character there can discover 3 skills via actions. But the third skill can never be taught to children via Educar Fills regardless of era, creating a silent cap inconsistency.
- **Reproduction**: In Edat Antiga, discover 3 skills. Attempt Educar Fills â€” only 2 can be marked as teachable per child even though the era allows 3.
- **Impact**: Minor dynasty progression ceiling; players in Era 3 lose one inheritance slot they would expect from the era's raised cap.
- **Fix**: Replace hard-coded `2` with `currentEra().mechanics.maxLearnedSkills` (or the target child's era's value).
- **Route to**: `gameplay-programmer`
